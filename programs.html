What We Do

We, at Whiteboard Consultants, believe new-age programs and up-skilling are the way forward in New India. Be it School going students, graduates or job seekers must be aware of Emerging Technologies. The New Education Policy (NEP 2020) also gives much importance to building a Digital India. Hence, exposure to Emerging Technologies for School Students is the way forward and COE is being set up to facilitate this. Similarly, for Graduates/Job Seekers, there lies a huge opportunity in the field of Investment Banking for Finance Graduates. Students with Engineering Degrees and Graduates with Science, Economics, Mathematics, and Statistics careers as Data Analysts, Data Scientists, and Cybersecurity Specialists have good prospects. Students with a flair for Embedded Technologies can also build up a great career in their field by picking up the required industry-specific skill sets. Students who wish to make a career in Additive Manufacturing and designing are also not left behind. They can learn 3D printing and transform the way products are designed.

A career in Sports also cannot be far behind. With the corporatisation of sports heralding the cricket leagues for Women and Men in the form of IPL, Football, with ISL has become more popular and does not remain a sport having a fan base only in a few states, but has become a national event. Kabaddi, Hockey are also not far behind in popularity. The Khelo India initiative has now brought sports into the lives of every student and the future of sports is only looking brighter in India. World is recognising Indian Athletes, Boxers, and Wrestlers today. Tennis, Badminton also are throwing big names in the world arena. The popularity and the corporatisation of sports have opened up newer careers for aspirants in the form of Sports Management, Coaching and Sports Science.
