Sports Management

Go back

Individuals who are passionate about sports and possess enterprise and expertise will make a big difference in the Sports industry. The best way in which one’s passion can be merged with one’s profession is by pursuing formal education in sports management, which would blend classroom learning with practical learning, in the form of live exposure.

Bachelor’s Degree in Sports Management (BSM)
Mumbai University

Sports Management as a career is challenging but immensely satisfying. The Sports industry, which is well on its way to becoming one of the biggest industries in the country, will accept individuals who want to make a difference either on or off the field, with open arms.

ISM’s Bachelor’s Degree in Sports Management program comes in two-fold prepping you for the industry just the way it should. The course is specially designed to provide you with specialisation into:
  • Management
  • Coaching

3 Years Full-Time Program

  • Uniquely designed undergraduate program to meet the needs of modern-day sporting ecosystem
  • Offers specialisation into Coaching
  • Internship & placement opportunities
  • Research & Project Development
  • Campus life that encourages learning by doing

Master’s Degree in Sports Management (MSM)
Mumbai University

The industry today needs revolutionary leaders who’d uplift it & pin India on a global map as an emerging multi-sporting country.

India over the last decade, owing to the influx of sporting leagues, developing as a multi-sport nation across the globe. Underpinning this emerging industry are the business professionals who provide direction and leadership to sporting organisations across the country.

IISM’s Master’s Degree in Sports Management program aims at developing world class leaders who can handle and manage their roles with an understanding of the practices, strategies and the fundamentals of the sporting ecosystem and the scopes in the related sector by instilling the necessary business and leadership skills.

Whether it’s at the elite, semi-elite or recreational levels, the program prepares you to deliver on all strategic dimensions of the industry – in areas such as marketing and promotions, governance and management, facilities and events, as well as participation development.

Admissions at IISM

IISM is India’s first of its kind & pioneer in Sports Management Education. We aim at providing a deeper perspective to the ideas and practice of management to the students.

The stringent admission process at IISM aids in thorough scrutiny. The process has been specifically designed to accommodate students who can be transformed into astute thought leaders, ready to take on the challenges of the sports corporate world, both in India and abroad.

The admission process comprises of 2 stages that facilitates in understanding the candidate’s liking & awareness towards the sports industry & their acumen for the business side of sports.

International Institute of Sports Management follows a Holistic Admission Process in order to admit students in the Sports Management programs. This admission process is to assess an aspirant on various parameters giving him/her a fair opportunity to showcase their competence. Through this process, IISM intends to select students who have the right potential, attitude and aptitude for the Sports Management Industry. In accordance with the eligibility guidelines, an aspirant has to go through two stages to be securing admission at IISM.

The Admission Process at IISM comprises of Stage I and Stage II

Stage I:

  • Stage I of the Admission Process for Master’s Degree at IISM complete in two steps:
    1. Caselets

      2. For MSM is the first step in Stage I of the Admission Process. Here, an aspirant would receive 4 topics over an email. Each topic holds 20 marks for itself. Aspirants will be given two days to submit their Caselets back on the email Id: caselets@iismworld.in . Caselets comprise a total of 80 marks and would be evaluated by an expert invigilator.

      The Caselets are designed to extract and evaluate the best of knowledge of the aspirant. It consists of cases- that will give a fair idea on how aspirants think, their outlook towards circumstances, knowledge on current affairs and proficiency in making Plan of Action.
      Through Caselets, we intend to estimate how would aspirants react to or take an action on situations that he’d face in the rapidly evolving Sports Management sector. The test is specially designed for the students from any educational and geographical background, who have keen interest in sports and have desire to make a career in sports management. This exam is conducted online & candidates will have to appear for this round from home.

    2. SMAT & Overall Performance

      3. The Overall Performance considers the Academic score, Sports Achievements & Extra-curricular Activities for 20 marks.

  • Stage 1 of the Admission Process for Bachelor’s Degree at IISM complete in two steps:
    1. Essay

      2. Essay (for BSM) in Stage I is a step wherein aspirants will have to share an Essay on a particular topic given. This consist of 80 marks. Aspirants will be given two days to submit their essay on the email Id: essay@iismworld.in This will be evaluated by an expert invigilator.

    2. SMAT & Overall Performance

Stage II

This Stage will be an online, Video-Call based, Personal Interview for 120 marks.
The total weightage of Stage I and Stage II will be 300. Aspirants needs to score 60% cumulative of Stage I and Stage II in order to secure Admission at IISM.
The final offer letter will be reflected on the candidate’s dashboard.
Each candidate having passed Stage I & Stage II will be given one week to pay their fees and confirm their Admission at IISM.
