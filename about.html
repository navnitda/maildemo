About Us


At Whiteboard Consultants, we specialise in advising Corporates on Sales Performance Improvement, Customer Experience, Sales Strategy and provide On-job Manpower Training on various aspects of Sales.
We also counsel individuals in choosing the Right Career path, Interview Handling, Resume Writing, Social Media Profile Building and Up-skilling.
After gathering over 30 years of experience in the Education Industry and Edtech, Whiteboard Consultants started its journey in December 2022, partnering with leaders in Edtech and Education, involved in skilling and up-skilling with 100% Job Assured Certification Programs in the fields of Data Science, Data Analytics, Cybersecurity, Additive Manufacturing, Embedded Systems and IoT, Investment Banking, Full Stack Development and Digital Marketing.
For students who wish to pursue Bachelor programs in Sports Management and Science, we are there to help.

Our Founder
