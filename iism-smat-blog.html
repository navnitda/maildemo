Understanding SMAT: Sports Management Aptitude Test

SMAT is a comprehensive assessment that evaluates candidates' aptitude in-blog Mathematics and English, with a strong emphasis on their interest and knowledge about the sporting ecosystem. While the test includes a wide range of questions related to sports.SMAT comes with a 30% weightage for sports related questions.

The test is specially designed for the students from any educational and geographical background, who have keen interest in-blog sports and have desire to make a career in-blog sports management. This exam is conducted online and candidates can give the exam from the comfort of their homes.
