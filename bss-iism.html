Bachelor’s Degree in Sports Science (BSS)
Mumbai University

Sports Science as a career is challenging but immensely satisfying. The Sports industry, which is well on its way to becoming one of the biggest industries in the country, will accept individuals who want to make a difference either on or off the field, with open arms.

This curriculum will assist participants in achieving career and personal goals by allowing them to participate in a competitive sector.
Course is curated by top sports scientists and is delivered by sports science practitioners

3 Years Full-Time Program

  • Recognised by the University Of Mumbai
  • Uniquely designed undergraduate program to meet the needs of modern-day sporting ecosystem
  • More than 50% of the entire course delivery is through live practicals
  • Practicals delivery at best labs, gyms, and training centers in Mumbai
  • Course delivered by medical practitioners, industry experts & top sports scientists
  • Newest career option for sports enthusiast wanting to make career in Science field
  • Course imparted by India’s pioneer institute in Sports Education, IISM
  • Internship & Placement Assistance
  • Field Visits

The basic criteria to be eligible to enroll in this course is 10+2(Science) Passed with Physics, Chemistry, Biology of any State Board/ CBSE/ ISC, or A-Level, IB, American 12th Grade or equivalent or any other examination recognized as equivalent thereto.

The Institute offers an internship at the end of the second year of BSS. The internship program provides the students with an opportunity to take a deeper dive into the sporting ecosystem, thus aiding them in understanding the business of sports & helping them grow their network.
The institute offers placement assistance at the end of the third year of BSS.

Master’s Degree in Sports Science (MSS)
Mumbai University

Master’s in Sports Science is a two-year full-time Master’s degree in Sports Science in collaboration with the University of Mumbai & GICED (Garware Institute of Career Education & Development).

This course will be conducted by the International Institute of Sports Management (IISM) which is a perfect combination of Classroom learning & Practical.

The learners get to apply advanced theory in practical projects as you collaborate with our network of industry partners. This will also enhance your professional prospects. The students are offered opportunities to learn & experience from live projects. The institute supports students to work on consultancy projects with sporting organizations & let them pursue their passion for sports science and excel in the profession.

Admissions at IISM

IISM is India’s first of its kind & pioneer in Sports Science Education. We aim at providing a deeper perspective to the ideas and practice of management to the students.

The stringent admission process at IISM aids in thorough scrutiny. The process has been specifically designed to accommodate students who can be transformed into astute thought leaders, ready to take on the challenges of the sports corporate world, both in India and abroad.

The admission process comprises of 2 stages that facilitates in understanding the candidate’s liking & awareness towards the sports industry & their acumen for the business side of sports.

International Institute of Sports Science follows a Holistic Admission Process in order to admit students in the Sports Science programs. This admission process is to assess an aspirant on various parameters giving him/her a fair opportunity to showcase their competence. Through this process, IISM intends to select students who have the right potential, attitude and aptitude for the Sports Science Industry. In accordance with the eligibility guidelines, an aspirant has to go through two stages to be securing admission at IISM.

The Admission Process at IISM comprises of Stage I and Stage II

Stage I:

  • Stage I of the Admission Process for Master’s Degree at IISM complete in two steps:
    1. Caselets

      2. For MSS is the first step in Stage I of the Admission Process. Here, an aspirant would receive 4 topics over an email. Each topic holds 20 marks for itself. Aspirants will be given two days to submit their Caselets back on the email Id: caselets@iismworld.in . Caselets comprise a total of 80 marks and would be evaluated by an expert invigilator.

      The Caselets are designed to extract and evaluate the best of knowledge of the aspirant. It consists of cases- that will give a fair idea on how aspirants think, their outlook towards circumstances, knowledge on current affairs and proficiency in making Plan of Action.
      Through Caselets, we intend to estimate how would aspirants react to or take an action on situations that he’d face in the rapidly evolving Sports Management sector. The test is specially designed for the students from any educational and geographical background, who have keen interest in sports and have desire to make a career in sports management. This exam is conducted online & candidates will have to appear for this round from home.

    2. SSAT & Overall Performance

      3. The Overall Performance considers the Academic score, Sports Achievements & Extra-curricular Activities for 20 marks.

  • Stage 1 of the Admission Process for Bachelor’s Degree at IISM complete in two steps:
    1. Essay

      2. Essay (for BSS) in Stage I is a step wherein aspirants will have to share an Essay on a particular topic given. This consist of 80 marks. Aspirants will be given two days to submit their essay on the email Id: essay@iismworld.in This will be evaluated by an expert invigilator.

    2. SSAT & Overall Performance

Stage II

This Stage will be an online, Video-Call based, Personal Interview for 120 marks.
The total weightage of Stage I and Stage II will be 300. Aspirants needs to score 60% cumulative of Stage I and Stage II in order to secure Admission at IISM.
The final offer letter will be reflected on the candidate’s dashboard.
Each candidate having passed Stage I & Stage II will be given one week to pay their fees and confirm their Admission at IISM.
