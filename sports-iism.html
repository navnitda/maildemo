Sports Management

Individuals who are passionate about sports and possess enterprise and expertise will make a big difference in the Sports industry. The best way in which one’s passion can be merged with one’s profession is by pursuing formal education in sports management, which would blend classroom learning with practical learning, in the form of live exposure.

Bachelor’s Degree in Sports Management (BSM)
Mumbai University

Sports Management as a career is challenging but immensely satisfying. The Sports industry, which is well on its way to becoming one of the biggest industries in the country, will accept individuals who want to make a difference either on or off the field, with open arms.

ISM’s Bachelor’s Degree in Sports Management program comes in two-fold prepping you for the industry just the way it should. The course is specially designed to provide you with specialisation into:
  • Management
  • Coaching

    • 3 Years Full-Time Program

  • Recognised by the University Of Mumbai
  • Uniquely designed undergraduate program to meet the needs of modern-day sporting ecosystem
  • Offers specialisation into Coaching
  • Internship & placement opportunities
  • Research & Project Development
  • Campus life that encourages learning by doing

    • Must have completed Class XII with a minimum aggregate of 50% for the Open Category & 45% for the reserved category in all the subjects. Read more here.

    The Institute offers an internship at the end of the second year of BSM. The internship program provides the students with an opportunity to take a deeper dive into the sporting ecosystem, thus aiding them in understanding the business of sports & helping them grow their network.
    The institute offers placement assistance at the end of the third year of BSM.

    Some of the exciting subjects that you will learn in a Bachelor’s Degree in Sports Management are: Financial Accounting, Business Statistics, Product Innovations Management, Introduction to Sports Coaching, Sports Marketing, Sports Broadcasting & Journalism among others.
